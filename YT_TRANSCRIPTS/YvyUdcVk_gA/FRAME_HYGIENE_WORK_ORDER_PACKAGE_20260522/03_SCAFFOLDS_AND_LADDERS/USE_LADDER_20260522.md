# Use Ladder

Step 1 - Intake
Hold the transcript as source material only.

Step 2 - Diagnose
Identify whether the useful idea concerns identity, control, safety, frame, process, or boundary.

Step 3 - Translate
Convert the idea into Clean Seed work-order language.

Step 4 - Place
Choose the correct lane:
- source ore
- candidate
- Soft Suit
- proof note
- active rule
- parked item

Step 5 - Prove
Do not promote without a live use, receipt, and clean boundary.

Step 6 - Guard
Block dirty uses: manipulation, dominance, hidden framing, or people-control.

Step 7 - Re-enter
Use only when work fog, lane drift, authority confusion, or proof instability appears.
