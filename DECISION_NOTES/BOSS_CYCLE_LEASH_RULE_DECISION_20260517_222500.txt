TITLE: Boss Cycle Leash Rule Decision

ISSUE:
The house had related pieces for weakest-link scans, suit wearing, tool trial, and adopt gate, but did not clearly define the user's requested boss/fight/wear/walk-around cycle as one operating rule.

DECISION:
Install Boss Cycle Leash as an active operating complement.

RULE:
Boss = current biggest proven weakness.
Fight = smallest clean fix.
Wear the fix = after proof, integrate the fix into the suit for the next scan.
Walk around = inspect the house after wearing the fix to test load, neighbor lanes, authority, proof, and anchor.
Next boss = weakness revealed after the walk-around.
Leash = straight-and-narrow path that prevents freedom from becoming drift.

NO-DRIFT BOUNDARY:
This complements the core. It does not change, reform, overwrite, or drift the core.
