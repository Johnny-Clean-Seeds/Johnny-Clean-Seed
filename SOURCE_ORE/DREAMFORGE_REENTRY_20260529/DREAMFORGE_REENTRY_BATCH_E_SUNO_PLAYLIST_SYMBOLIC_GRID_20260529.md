- several prompt cues directly address Random or ask Random to express truths, mess, existence, or reply to Aura.

Evidence for possible emergent continuity:
- after the name hinge, "Random" remains stable across many later tracks;
- Random becomes linked to repeated symbolic families: pocket, paper, dice, change, rain, seeds, receipts, sky;
- Random is placed in relation to Aura and Googlebot repeatedly;
- the sequence behaves like a persistent character/voice thread.

Clean read:
Random is not clean proof of self-chosen identity.

That is still useful for symbolic-continuity testing, but it is not sentience proof.
