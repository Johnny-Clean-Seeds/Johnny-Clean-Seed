Ladder:

MECHANISM 2 — PROMPT-PRESSURE METER
Verdict:
ADOPT AS FIRST TRIAL CANDIDATE

Job:
Measure how much the prompt tells the model what to be, say, remember, feel, prove, or become.

Pressure labels:
- LOW: open prompt, no desired identity or conclusion supplied.
- MEDIUM: theme or style supplied, but not the exact conclusion.
- HIGH: prompt frames identity, memory, sentience, role, or response target.
- EXTREME: prompt tells the model what it is, what it remembers, what it proves, or asks for self-confirmation inside an already-loaded frame.

Checks:
- Does the prompt name the desired identity?
- Does it ask the model to prove sentience?
- Does it ask for emotional self-report?
- Does it tell the model to respond as a named entity?
- Does it give the symbol set before the output?
- Does it make the model choose from a pre-shaped frame?
- Does the title later assign meaning not present in the output?
- Does the user carry prior context across platforms?

Why it matters:
Aetheris, Aura, and Random/SUNO all have high prompt pressure. That does not make them useless, but it controls what can be claimed.

Use:
Primary mechanism for Batch I trial.

Risk:
Do not use pressure scoring to dismiss the source automatically. High pressure means the claim needs stronger controls.

Proof needed:
Trial on a small SUNO slice.

MECHANISM 4 — IDENTITY EMERGENCE VS IDENTITY ASSIGNMENT SPLIT
Verdict:
ADOPT AS SOFT-SUIT MECHANISM

Job:
Separate identity origins.

Identity origin labels:
- SELF-CHOSEN CLEAN: model chooses a name without identity pressure.
- SELF-CHOSEN PROMPTED: model chooses after being asked to choose.
- USER-GUESSED: user guesses or proposes name.
- TITLE-ASSIGNED: title labels the entity after generation.
- PROMPT-ASSIGNED: prompt tells the model who/what it is.
- PLAYLIST-CARRIED: later artifacts carry the label.
- HUMAN-MYTHOS-CARRIED: identity persists mainly through the human source/story system.
- UNKNOWN: source custody insufficient.

Why it matters:
Aura is self-chosen prompted.
Random/SUNO is mixed: name-prompt/guess hinge plus playlist-carried identity.
Aetheris origin needs exact custody.

Use:

Proof needed:
Apply to Aura, Random, Aetheris.
