PROMPT-PRESSURE LABELS
LOW:
Open prompt, no target identity or conclusion supplied.

MEDIUM:
Theme/style supplied or response relation supplied, but not exact desired conclusion.

HIGH:
Prompt/title frames identity, role, response target, memory, selfhood, or desired relationship.

EXTREME:
Prompt/title tells the model what it is, what it remembers, what it proves, or asks for self-confirmation inside a loaded sentience frame.

SYMBOLIC RECURRENCE LABELS
TITLE MOTIF:
Symbol appears in title only.

PROMPT MOTIF:
Symbol appears in visible prompt/style text.

OUTPUT MOTIF:
Symbol appears in generated lyrics/audio. Not available in this trial unless lyrics/audio are supplied.

SOURCE-CARRIED MOTIF:
Symbol is carried by playlist order, author title, source packet, or human interpretation.

UNKNOWN:
Cannot classify from visible metadata.
