PUBLIC_NOTES/

DUPLICATE SURFACE RULE

If a guide file appears both at root and in ACTIVE_GUIDES/, the ACTIVE_GUIDES copy is the active packet copy unless CURRENT_TRUTH_INDEX.txt proves a different current authority. Root copies are public mirrors/reference surfaces.

4. PUBLIC_NOTES, TEST_LANES, and Shed are labeled non-authority by default.
5. Root guide copies are labeled mirror/reference unless proven current.
