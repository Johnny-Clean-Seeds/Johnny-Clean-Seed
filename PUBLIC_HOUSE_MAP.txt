HOME / PUBLIC COEXISTENCE RULE

The repo home is the assistant/model brain for this build.

It may be publicly reachable, but public reach does not define its purpose or authority.

Use home, house, or brain language when judging function, routing, authority, proof flow, memory, and word keys.

Use public language only when describing exposure, access boundary, or named lanes such as PUBLIC_NOTES.

Do not treat human-facing polish as a weakness unless it affects assistant entry, routing, authority clarity, proof flow, memory, or word-key precision.

Shed/

ROOT POINTER RULE

If a guide file appears both at root and in ACTIVE_GUIDES/, the ACTIVE_GUIDES copy is the active packet copy unless CURRENT_TRUTH_INDEX.txt proves a different current authority.

Root guide files are pointer/reference surfaces.

They route the assistant to the active guide packet.

They are not mirror doctrine bodies.

WORD-KEY RULE

Words are keys.

Use the most accurate word for the role:

- Home: the repo brain/house used for assistant entry, memory, routing, proof, and action.
- Public: exposure/access boundary, not purpose or authority.
- Active guide: can guide behavior inside its lane.
- Pointer: routes to another file.
- Reference: supports understanding.
- Evidence: supports a claim but does not command by volume.
- Source-ore: parked material that may be mined later.
- Test lane: controlled experiment area.
- Public note: support material unless deliberately promoted.
