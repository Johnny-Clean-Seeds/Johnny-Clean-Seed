## Combined Working Model

The best current structure is not cube alone, atom alone, or rings alone.

It is:

`ringed grammar around a nucleus, with cube/semilattice used as creative topology`

Plain version:

- Nucleus: active value, active boss, authority, proof burden.
- Inner ring: parent gates that decide, block, permit, route, prove, sequence, or close.
- Middle ring: checkpoints, helpers, tools, cards, ledgers, adapters.
- Outer ring: source ore, symbolic ideas, weak signals, research, creative room, parking.
- Cube / semilattice overlay: shows possible neighbor relations and all-ways pressure.
- Grammar: controls legal moves so every possible relation does not become an active relation.
