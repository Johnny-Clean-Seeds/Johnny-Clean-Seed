6. Collision prevention rule
Do not restore Recycle Bin items to original paths.
Do not restore Recycle Bin items into one flat folder.
Do not merge same-name files.
Do not overwrite existing files.

Same-name files are expected. Duplicate names such as README_START_HERE.txt, CLEAN_SEED_WRAP_GUIDE.txt, CLEAN_SEED_ALIGNMENT_CHECK.txt, EVIDENCE_ARCHIVE_MANIFEST.txt, and PATCH_RECEIPT groups must remain separated by item number and quarantine folder.

Do not execute shortcuts.
Do not extract zip files during recovery.
