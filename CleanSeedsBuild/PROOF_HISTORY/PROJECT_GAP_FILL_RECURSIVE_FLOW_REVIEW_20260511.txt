STATION CARD V0 - SMALL OPERATING BRIDGE

Use this when the full architecture stack is too large.

AUTHORITY
Receives: project root, source files, user request.
May do: identify active truth, local bridge limits, support proof lanes.
Must not do: treat memory or proof-history as active truth.
Proof needed: exact paths, hashes for active files, patch/state IDs.
Output: authority boundary.
Stop if: authority source conflicts or is missing.

ACTIVE BALL
Receives: current target.
May do: name the one item in hand.
Must not do: silently switch tasks.
Proof needed: target, owner, status, next gate.
Output: one active ball.
Stop if: more than one active ball is uncontrolled.

FREEZE
Receives: active ball plus review/risk need.
May do: stop movement and state change.
Must not do: edit, move, delete, rename, overwrite, cleanup, promote, run scripts.
Proof needed: what is frozen and what is protected.
Output: stable scene.
Stop if: state cannot be protected.

CAPTURE / COLLECT
Receives: frozen target.
May do: preserve visible evidence.
Must not do: judge, fix, clean, move, delete, or promote.
Proof needed: paths, names, sizes, hashes when needed, timestamps, logs, receipts.
Output: raw evidence.
Stop if: evidence is too thin for inventory.

INVENTORY
Receives: captured evidence.
May do: turn it into a structured list.
Must not do: judge, delete, or promote.
Proof needed: name, path, role, type, source, status, evidence present/missing.
Output: inventory list.
Stop if: identity or source is unclear.

SORT
Receives: inventory list.
May do: group by role, source, risk, authority, status, next gate.
Must not do: decide value or fix.
Proof needed: pile criteria.
Output: clean piles.
Stop if: grouping hides different risk or authority.

JUDGE
Receives: sorted item or pile.
May do: decide value, risk, authority, next gate.
Must not do: perform the action.
Proof needed: evidence, comparison, affected neighbors, unknowns.
Output: provisional decision.
Stop if: approval, proof, or scope is missing.

PLACE / FILE
Receives: judged item.
May do: route to proof-history, parked, blocked, review, candidate, active-guide-review lane.
Must not do: promote by placement.
Proof needed: lane reason and reopen/next gate.
Output: findable status.
Stop if: lane is unproven.

SAVE
Receives: approved save target.
May do: create proof-history file/receipt/hash record.
Must not do: overwrite, move, delete, promote, or edit active files unless separately approved.
Proof needed: exact path, reason, boundary.
Output: saved evidence.
Stop if: path exists unexpectedly or approval is unclear.

SEAL
Receives: saved/reviewed item.
May do: record path, size, hash, status, no-change boundary.
Must not do: turn save success into final PASS.
Proof needed: file size, hash, protected-state confirmations.
Output: sealed proof state.
Stop if: verification fails.

REVIEW / HANDOFF
Receives: sealed item or bounded review target.
May do: inspect, challenge, recommend.
Must not do: final PASS, promote, or override authority.
Proof needed: evidence used, clean parts, weak parts, next gate.
Output: provisional review.
Stop if: reviewer authority or evidence is unclear.

PROMOTE / PARK / BLOCK
Receives: reviewed item.
May do: set next state.
Must not do: promote without separate approval or delete by judgment.
Proof needed: approval, park reason, block reason, reopen condition.
Output: clear state.
Stop if: user decision or proof is missing.

CONTINUE
Receives: closed state.
May do: return to authority/active-ball check.
Must not do: drift into unrelated work.
Proof needed: next clean action.
Output: next cycle.
Stop if: an open ball remains.
