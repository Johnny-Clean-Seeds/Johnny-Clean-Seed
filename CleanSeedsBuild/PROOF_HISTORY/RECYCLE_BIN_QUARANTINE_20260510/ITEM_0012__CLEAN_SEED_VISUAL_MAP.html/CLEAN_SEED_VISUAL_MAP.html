Clean Seed Visual Map

A simple view of the system: the build starts small, words control the route, evidence decides trust, dirty milk gets quarantined, and growth only happens after a clean close.
Clean Seed is a proof cycle: control the words, certify the files, quarantine dirty milk, prove the right cause, then close before growth.
1. TargetName the exact point. What are we building, judging, fixing, saving, or proving?
2. AuthoritySay who owns direction, what may be touched, and what is out of bounds.
3. ObjectName the exact file, rule, receipt, command, folder, artifact, or claim.
4. ActionOnly do the smallest safe move: check, patch, save, park, yield, block, or close.
5. EvidenceList what proves the claim, what would disprove it, and what evidence lane is missing.
6. RiskRisk decides evidence burden. High risk means preserve first and verify before action.
7. IncidentBefore suspect hunting, ask whether the incident even happened: real, false, stale, partial, or unknown.
8. SuspectFind the right cause, not the easy cause. Weak “may mean” claims stay unproven.
9. VerdictDone is not PASS. PASS needs proof. INVALID means the evidence cannot prove it.
10. Next StateClose, park, yield, quarantine, promote, or loop back to target.
Final check: if the next state no longer matches the starting target, the path drifted.

Dirty Milk Quarantine

Questionable input, files, receipts, rules, commands, claims, evidence, or ideas are held out of active trust until checked. Dirty milk can appear. It must not be carried forward.

Evidence Murder Board

First prove there is a body. Then prove it was murder. Then test suspects. The goal is not any suspect; it is the right suspect, supported by evidence.

Growth After Wrap

The seed grows only after the current loop is named, bounded, proven, closed, and ready. Nothing promotes itself. Future ideas wait in the right lane.
