Mom Logic

She does not trust loose scraps. She tucks the useful ones away, labels the rest, and keeps the warm notes where she can feel them when she walks.

Not Too Technical

The pocket is not a server. It just happens to remember what matters, reject messy inputs, and keep the good route close to her hip.

Pocket Warning
