# Fog Zone / Unproven Source Ore Rule

Status: SUPPORT RULE / UNCERTAINTY HANDLING  
Date: 2026-05-23  
Scope: news, current ideas, weak signals, little-guy sources, spiritual/symbolic material, early pattern matches

## Core meaning

Fog Zone says:

"Look, I still don't know everything."

Fog Zone is not trash.

Fog Zone is not truth.

Fog Zone is the lane for material that may matter but is not yet proved.

## Goes here

- news;
- current ideas;
- weak signals;
- little-guy sources;
- spiritual/symbolic material;
- unproven claims;
- early pattern matches;
- hot but unclear concepts;
- outside chatter that may contain signal.

## Required handling

Bring it in as candidate signal.

Then apply:
1. source custody;
2. evidence rating;
3. lane fit;
4. poison/hazard check;
5. neighbor check;
6. proof lane;
7. small test;
8. moving-forward use;
9. adopt / adapt / park / block.

## Moving Forward

From now on, I will not throw away unproven material just because it is not proven or not from a big authority.

I will also not treat unproven material as fact.

This is better because it keeps the house open to live signal without letting unknown material poison the house.

## Short form

Read widely.

Believe carefully.

Use only what proves fit.

Fog means proceed carefully, not stop seeing.
