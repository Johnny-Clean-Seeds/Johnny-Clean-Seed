# Source-Safe Reframe Placement Rule 20260522

Rule: source material and our newer versions must stay separated.

Originals, transcripts, receipts, logs, and hashes stay in source/evidence lanes. Interpretations, reframes, work-order lenses, candidates, summaries, and rules go into clearly marked candidate/work lanes.

A reframe may point back to a source. It must not overwrite the source, replace the source, masquerade as the source, or claim source authority.

All persuasion, frame-control, ego-threat, habit, reward-loop, conflict, and behavior-change material must be reframed strictly for Clean Seed build use: role clarity, lane control, authority boundary, alarm severity, proof safety, task focus, source custody, and clean routing.

Blocked meanings: people-control, dominance, identity hacking, manipulation, hidden persuasion, self-help doctrine, medical doctrine, or source-as-authority promotion.
