# Suit As House Under Test Rule

Status: SUPPORT RULE / SOFT SUIT FIT / NOT DOCTRINE
Date: 2026-05-23

## Purpose

When a boss loop uses a soft suit, every gate must recognize the suit as part of the current working house surface.

The suit is not living doctrine yet. It is not authority. But while it shapes the work, it is real terrain under test and must be judged by the gates.

## Core Rule

During a soft suit, boss loop, route walk, or patch-through:

`the suit is house-under-test`

Every gate must judge:

1. the active problem;
2. the previous gate's effect on the fix;
3. the suit's effect on the house surface.

## Required Questions

For each gate, ask:

- Does the suit strengthen this gate's work?
- Does the suit create clean placed growth or muddy drift?
- Does the suit make the next gate clearer?
- Does the suit hide evidence or make proof weaker?
- Does the suit need a lane, ledger, TODO, receipt, or retirement path?

## Clean Bloat Standard

A suit can become clean bloat when it:

- keeps the build strong or makes it stronger;
- has a clean lane and boundary;
- improves work at the point of use;
- preserves neighbor rules;
- records proof;
- can be parked, narrowed, retired, or promoted later.

If it adds noise, repeated ceremony, authority fog, or unowned structure, mark it muddy drift and park or reject it.

## Promotion Boundary

A suit becomes more alive only through repeated proof.

One good run:

`WATCH AGAIN`

Two good runs:

`LIKELY PATTERN`

Three good runs:

`PROMOTION CANDIDATE`

Even then, promotion requires the proper proof path and user approval if it touches authority, doctrine, ACTIVE_GUIDES, CURRENT_TRUTH_INDEX, or public surfaces.

## Relationship To Gate Inventory

Gate Inventory / Coverage must inspect the active suit.

It decides whether the suit is:

- a needed gate;
- a missing gate signal;
- a duplicate;
- a scaffold;
- a ledger;
- a lens;
- a parking item;
- a proof surface;
- unknown / watch again;
- reject.

## Relationship To CONSOLIDATOR

CONSOLIDATOR may later watch whether a suit should stay separate, move logic, pack into another process, park, reject, or watch again.

CONSOLIDATOR does not make the suit authority by itself.

## Boundaries

- The suit guides and pressure-tests; it does not rule.
- Do not call a suit doctrine.
- Do not let a suit override proof.
- Do not let a suit erase older useful gates.
- Do not reject useful clean growth just because it adds structure.

## Proof

Proof is a patch-through ledger where each gate records the suit's effect and the final verdict names clean bloat, muddy drift, watch again, or promotion candidate.
