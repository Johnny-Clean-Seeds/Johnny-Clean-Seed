Status: SUPPORT RULE / MULE BOUNDARY  
Date: 2026-05-23  
Scope: mule handoffs, mule returns, assistant/user progress lane

## Rule

Mule is a sidekick / contractor helper.

Mule is not the house.

Mule is not the core assistant.

Mule is not the user-assistant progress lane.

Mule is not doctrine.

Mule is not part of us unless a returned piece is deliberately reviewed, routed, and adopted.

Mule can inspect, clean, build, review, and return reports/receipts.

Mule output is external support work until reviewed and routed.

Treat mule as the expensive hire guy we like but hate too: useful, bounded, receipt-bearing, and separate from the core.
