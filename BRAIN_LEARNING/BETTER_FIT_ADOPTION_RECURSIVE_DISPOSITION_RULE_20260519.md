# Better-Fit Adoption / Recursive Disposition Rule

Date: 2026-05-19
Base: main @ 56c2eb3
Lane: Brain Learning
Status: support learning rule, not active guide doctrine

## Rule

When a new idea is cleaner, fits better, shares better with neighboring rules/tools, reduces drag, or produces better proof, adopt the new way through the correct gates.

Do not keep an old idea active out of loyalty.

Do not crown a new idea because it feels exciting.

## Old idea handling

The old idea is not deleted by reflex.

Set it aside with a clear disposition:

- parked/source-ore,
- superseded,
- helper-only,
- historical,
- rejected-as-active,
- not-yet,
- needs proof.

Old ideas may remain useful as support, contrast, source-ore, or future pattern material.

## Required move labels

Every material move must name:

- Action: what changed.
- Reason: why it changed.
- Result: what happened.
- Proof: what shows it worked, or why it is only a candidate.
- Disposition: applied, adapted, tested, parked/source-ore, rejected, not-yet, or needs proof.

## Result talk

After each important move, state:

- what got better,
- what did not get better,
- what remains unproven,
- what should become the better way if it keeps passing.

## No loose ends

Do not leave action gaps.

Do not leave a mule report half-used.

Do not abandon an old idea without disposition.

Do not let a new idea steer without placement, proof, and boundary.

## Recursive style / one-way path

Recursive is the style and flow.

One-way is the controlled path:

source
-> candidate
-> test
-> proof
-> disposition
-> promotion only if earned.

The whirlpool / generator / Tesla-coil image means the house should become self-sustaining by cycling proof into cleaner future motion.

It does not mean endless looping.

It does not mean gate-skipping.

If the circuit is wrong, the power burns the house.
If the circuit is right, proof becomes cleaner routing power.

## Boundary

This rule does not authorize:

- doctrine rewrite,
- active guide rewrite,
- CURRENT_TRUTH_INDEX change,
- tool promotion,
- Soft Suit promotion,
- automation,
- full lesson index,
- knowledge graph,
- dashboard.

It only controls adoption and disposition discipline.
