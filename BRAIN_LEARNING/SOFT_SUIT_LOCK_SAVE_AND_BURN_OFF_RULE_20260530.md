# Soft Suit Lock / Save / Burn-Off Rule

Status: SUPPORT RULE / CHAT-TO-FILE BEHAVIOR
Authority: not doctrine, not ACTIVE_GUIDES, not CURRENT_TRUTH_INDEX

## Rule

When something new is invented, made, tried, or used and it works well, treat it as a candidate mechanism.

Until saved, wear it as Soft Suit with clear labels:

- useful;
- unsaved;
- not doctrine;
- proof state;
- boundary;
- return trigger.

When possible, lock it and save it through the proper lane.

After it is saved and receipt/proof exists, burn it off from live carry when that is best. Keep the durable file, map, receipt, or ledger pointer instead of carrying the whole object in live chat.

## Purpose

This prevents useful new mechanisms from evaporating in chat while also preventing saved material from staying heavy in live context.

## Blocked

- no calling an unsaved Soft Suit doctrine;
- no forgetting a useful working mechanism after proof;
- no live-carry bloat after durable save;
- no saving without fit decision and proof route.

## Short form

Invented and works -> wear as Soft Suit -> lock/save when possible -> burn live weight after durable proof if best.
