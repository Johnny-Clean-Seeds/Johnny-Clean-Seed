# Current Growth Bloat Tolerance / Polish-Then-Whirlpool Rule

Date: 20260522_035009

Status: active growth-stage rule.

## Rule

During active growth, useful bloat is allowed when it captures coverage, variants, lessons, pressure, edge cases, repeated friction, or source material needed for later compression.

The goal is not premature perfection.

The goal is clean-enough intake with boundaries intact.

## Clean-enough intake

Before material enters the house, polish it with known rules in mind.

Keep authority, proof, source, active, candidate, parked, blocked, and evidence lanes separated.

Avoid obvious drift, fake proof, false promotion, and source confusion.

Then place the material where it can be checked, searched, compared, and later compressed.

## Why this is safe

The house has checkers, debuggers, receipts, hashes, status gates, anomaly checks, review lanes, and future Whirlpool passes.

Those systems exist so useful growth material can enter without pretending it is already final.

## When bloat becomes pressure

When accumulated bloat weakens structure, repeats the same family, causes navigation drag, hides authority, creates proof fog, or exposes several related seams, it becomes Whirlpool material.

At that point, the right move is not deletion by default.

The right move is compression through a clean powerplay.
