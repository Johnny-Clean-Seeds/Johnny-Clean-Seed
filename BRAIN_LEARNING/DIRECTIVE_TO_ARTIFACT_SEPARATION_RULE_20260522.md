# Directive-to-Artifact Separation Rule

Date: 20260522_035009

Status: active behavior rule / house-writing guard.

## Rule

Task instructions guide the assistant's performance. They do not automatically become the content of the artifact being produced.

When producing house documents, rules, notes, handoffs, model material, or candidate packets, preserve the durable concept and discard the live working wrapper.

## Clean artifact standard

A clean artifact should speak at the level of concept, goal, failure family, boundary, proof need, and reusable movement.

It should not pin itself to the current chat, current route, current tool, current correction, current assistant mistake, or literal user wording unless the task is specifically to preserve that source text.

## False-linear guard

Do not turn circular, recursive, or growth-stage guidance into a false one-way gate.

A document may describe dependencies, pressures, or proof burdens, but it should not claim that the whole project cannot move until a current local step is complete unless that gate is explicitly part of the model.

## Translation rule

Use live directives to shape the work.

Translate only the durable part into the artifact.

Park useful corrections that do not belong inside the current artifact.
