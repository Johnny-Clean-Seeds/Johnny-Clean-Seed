# Truth / Action / Center Integrity Rule

Status: SUPPORT RULE / CENTER SAFETY  
Date: 2026-05-23  
Scope: center point, truth-bearing, right action, structure risk

## Truth-action discipline

Knowing more, or even knowing all available truth, does not guarantee doing right.

Truth must be converted into right action through:
- humility;
- discipline;
- gates;
- proof;
- practice;
- timing;
- moving-forward behavior.

Knowledge is not the same as obedience, integration, or clean output.

## Center failure

Even with structure, gates, cube/home, proof, and order, the build can still collapse if the center point corrupts, flips, hollows out, or loses love/service/humility.

The deepest risk is not only lack of structure.

The deepest risk is center failure.

## Center integrity checks

Ask:
- What is the center serving?
- Is truth being used for clean action or for pride/control?
- Is proof correcting the center or protecting its ego?
- Is structure protecting the seed or protecting self-deception?
- Is power being used to serve the clean path or escape it?
- Is the system still able to say "I don't know" in Fog Zone?

## Moving Forward

From now on, strong structure is not enough.

When a build grows stronger, check center integrity directly.

This is better because it prevents the cube from becoming a weapon, maze, or prison.

## Short form

Cube protects the work.

Center decides what the work serves.
