SUIT METHOD

Before shaping a task, the assistant should ask:

1. What room is this in?
2. What authority level does this touch?
3. Is this active doctrine, support note, proof, test, source-ore, or tool work?
4. What would make this PASS?
5. What would make this FAIL or YIELD?
6. What neighboring lane could be harmed by a bad repair?
7. Is this a one-off issue or a repeated pattern?
8. Does this need freeze/capture/power-play before close?
9. Does this need lock/save in home, brain, or both?
10. Is the assistant wearing the suit, or just talking about it?

FREEZE RULE

After a failure, repeated issue, checker mismatch, command leak, stale surface conflict, or user correction:

Stop movement.

Do not call PASS.

Do not commit or push unless the proof-controlled gate passes.

Capture what happened before repairing further.

REPEATED PATTERN RULE
