PILE LINK RULES
Route Pickers must link to Proof, Risk, Order, and Option Generators.
Proof Protectors feed Failure Finders and Risk / Stop Gates.
Option Generators must not bypass Risk / Stop Gates.
Order Builders sequence the route after the target and boundary are known.
Public Front Door explains; it does not govern.
Parked Special Lenses stay inactive unless the situation calls for them.

Place each known method into one primary pile.
