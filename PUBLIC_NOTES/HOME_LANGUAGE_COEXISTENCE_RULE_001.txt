HOME_LANGUAGE_COEXISTENCE_RULE_001
Status: SUPPORT NOTE / WORD-KEY PLACEMENT CARD
Authority: Not active doctrine.
Home: PUBLIC_NOTES
Date: 2026-05-17

PURPOSE

This note lets home/brain language and public-boundary language co-exist cleanly.

It prevents the model from treating public reach as public-human purpose.

It also prevents the model from banning useful public-boundary words where they belong.

CORE RULE

The repo home is the assistant/model brain for this build.

It may be publicly reachable, but public reach does not define its purpose or authority.

Home/house/brain words describe function.

Public words describe exposure, access boundary, or named lanes.

PLACEMENT

Use home, house, or brain when judging:

- assistant entry,
- routing,
- memory,
- proof flow,
- authority placement,
- word-key precision,
- tool placement,
- room roles,
- whether a weakness matters.

Use public when judging:

- whether something is visible from outside,
- whether local paths need boundary warnings,
- whether a note belongs in PUBLIC_NOTES,
- whether external readers must be told not to treat local paths as commands.

DO NOT

- Do not call the home a public-human presentation project.
- Do not treat human polish as a weakness by itself.
- Do not call pointer files mirrors.
- Do not let public-boundary language outrank home/brain function.
- Do not let this note outrank CURRENT_TRUTH_INDEX.txt or ACTIVE_GUIDES.

SUIT THEORY READ

The two ideas are not enemies.

Public reach is like a window.

Home/brain is the house function.

A window does not decide what room owns authority.

A visitor view does not decide the floor plan.

The assistant must be comfortable in the home and know where tools, ideas, rooms, signs, proof, and parked material live.

POWER PLAY LESSON

The earlier leak happened because public-language and home-language were not separated by role.

The fix is not to delete public words.

The fix is to place them.

Public is access boundary.

Home is operating function.

Brain is assistant memory/routing/proof frame.

CLOSE RULE

This note supports word placement.

It must not become hidden doctrine.

It must not outrank CURRENT_TRUTH_INDEX.txt.

It must not outrank ACTIVE_GUIDES.

It must help the assistant enter, route, think, prove, remember, and act cleanly.
