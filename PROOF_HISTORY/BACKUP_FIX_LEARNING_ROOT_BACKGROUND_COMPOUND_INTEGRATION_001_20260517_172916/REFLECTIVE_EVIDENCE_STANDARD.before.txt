REFLECTIVE EVIDENCE STANDARD

STATUS:
Working standard for learning reports.
Not active doctrine.

WHEN TO USE:
Use after adding, fixing, changing, testing, deep cleaning, expanding, moving, or saving anything that affects the working environment.

REQUIRED REPORT SHAPE:
Expected:
Before:
Changed:
Now:
Better because:
Risk reduced:
Heavier because:
Still unproven:
Reuse later:
Compounding lesson:

REPORT STANDARD:
When saying fix, changed, or changes made, always say:
- what changed
- what was weak or missing before
- what it is now
- why it is better
- what risk it reduces
- what remains unproven or limited

MAIN RULE:
These details are not clutter when they are placed.
They are learning evidence.
