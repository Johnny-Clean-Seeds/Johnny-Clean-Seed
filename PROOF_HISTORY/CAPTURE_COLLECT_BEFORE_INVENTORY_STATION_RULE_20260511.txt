Definitions:

FREEZE means stop movement and prevent state change.

CAPTURE / COLLECT means preserve the visible evidence while it is still untouched: paths, names, timestamps, hashes when needed, file lists, command output, visible status, receipts, logs, and current state.

INVENTORY means turn captured evidence into a structured list.

SORT means group like with like.

JUDGE means decide value, risk, authority, and next gate.

Dirty state:
