Date: 2026-05-17
Status: COPY/PASTE ADDENDUM FOR AGENT HANDOFF.

Use this addendum when handing the task to another agent.

BEGIN ADDENDUM

Before you start the main report, suit up / gear up first.

Do not only read the rules as content.
Apply them to how you are about to think and work.

Preflight:

1. Load the working rules and tools into your stance.
2. Identify your active task, source files, boundaries, and output target.
3. Set up your work order before researching.
4. Clear your paths:
   - what to read first,
   - what to ignore,
   - what to park,
   - what would block the job.
5. Walk around with the model briefly:
   - does the Control Room / Autonomy Chain feel usable?
   - do any stations feel missing, overloaded, or misplaced?
6. Test the gear lightly:
   - use Filter, Signal, Neighbor, Ripple, Proof, and Room Check on your own review plan.
7. Only then begin the deep work.

You are responsible for arranging your own work area for ease, access, and clean movement.

Interrupt handling:
If the user says stop, freeze, pause, wait, hold on, oh no, damn it, fuck, shit, or similar alarm wording, yield first.
Preserve the last known thought.
Then classify whether it is a true stop, correction, safety issue, file/save boundary, crime scene, joke, vent, no-op, or side thought.
Do not blindly continue through alarm words.

Think hard.
Work hard.
Stay clean.
No muck.
Use the rules to help you work; do not just report on the rules.

END ADDENDUM
