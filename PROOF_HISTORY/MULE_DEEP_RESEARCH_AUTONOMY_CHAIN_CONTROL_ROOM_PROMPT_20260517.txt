CURRENT MODEL TO ANALYZE

The current working idea is:

The assistant should not merely have rules.
Rules must become trigger-based routes that fire the right station, latch, proof stamp, or self-fix when the situation appears.

Autonomy here does not mean wild freedom.
Autonomy means bounded self-movement inside a safe house.

Soft movement is allowed:
- test,
- rearrange,
- scaffold,
- park,
- compare,
- self-fix,

- Git saves,
- active-guide installs,
- cleanup/delete/move/rename,
- risky commands,
- authority changes.
