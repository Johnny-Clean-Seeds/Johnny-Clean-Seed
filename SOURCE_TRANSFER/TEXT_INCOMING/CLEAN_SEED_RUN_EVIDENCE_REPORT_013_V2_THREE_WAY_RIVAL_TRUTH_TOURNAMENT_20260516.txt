STING CORE LINE

WHAT STING ADDS
STING asks:
- What if all current models are wrong?
- What contradiction is everyone avoiding?
- What hidden assumption makes the winner look good?
- What would make this whole tournament invalid?
- What evidence would force the Judge to reject all three?
- Is the winner true, or merely useful?
- Is the model solving the real problem, or only the problem it is good at seeing?
