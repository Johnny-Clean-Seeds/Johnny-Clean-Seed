## Source-use boundary

These sources are not house authority.

They are comparison surfaces for understanding common sense as:

- practical judgment;
- shared background;
- social/cultural system;
- tacit know-how;
- affordance perception;
- bounded rationality;
- legal reasonableness;
- common ground in language;
- failure-prone heuristic compression.

The house decides what to adopt, adapt, test, park, or reject.
