## Capture Group 12 - Dependency Mirage

Name:
Dependency Mirage

User signal:
Dependency is often a fog word or mirage. Need changes by task, time, care, context, and choice.

Capture:
Do not treat dependency as a fixed dirty state.

Ask:
- What kind of need is this?
- Is it chosen?
- Is it mutual?
- Does it increase agency or reduce it?
- Does it trap the person or make them able to move?
- Does it create care, clarity, proof, and return?

Clean test:
The issue is not whether something is needed. The issue is what the need does to agency, burden, freedom, care, and movement.

State:
Fog-word / neighbor-force theory.

Authority:
Source-pattern / word-key candidate.

## Capture Group 13 - Fog Word Placement Check

Name:
Fog Word Placement Check

User signal:
The problem is not always the word or its dictionary meaning. The placement is messed up.

Capture:
Loaded words become damaging when placed as:
- master diagnosis
- shame label
- final verdict
- authority weapon
- fog wall
- conversation ender
- agency thief

Clean placement:
- signal
- context
- neighbor force
- hypothesis
- pattern to inspect
- temporary label
- question starter

Check questions:
- Where was this word placed?
- Who placed it there?
- What authority is it pretending to have?
- What does it block?
- What does it permit?
- Does it explain the structure or hide it?
- Does it increase clean movement or steal agency?

Repair:
Do not ban the word. Pull it out of the wrong throne, inspect its real job, put it in the right lane, remove fake authority, and test what it proves.

State:
Strong word-key candidate.

Authority:
Likely future support rule.
