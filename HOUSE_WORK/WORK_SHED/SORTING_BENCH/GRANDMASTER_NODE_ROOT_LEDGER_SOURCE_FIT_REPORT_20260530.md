## Source authority rule

External sources are support ore and method-fit evidence. They do not become house authority by themselves. Adopted pieces must be translated into house-native grammar, bounded, tested, and routed through proof.

V1.1 repairs this by separating ManifestAuditCandidates from ForceAddCandidates: content files are audited first, then the manifest is written, then the receipt is written, then exact-file staging verifies the final set.
