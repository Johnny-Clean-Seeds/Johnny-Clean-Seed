## Why this fits

The current front-door system already says:

- enter through a current pointer;
- do not carry the whole proof pile;
- follow only needed tunnels;
- use toolbelts;
- preserve boundaries.

Organic Pathing adds the missing living layer:

- repeated movement becomes evidence;
- helpers must return path-usable work;
- intake must classify and join material before promotion;
- shortcuts must be judged, not blindly paved;
- paths must include role, proof, boundary, and return.

## Helper fit

Helpers should be used as route testers and return-path witnesses, not as loose idea generators.

A helper output is acceptable only when it includes:

- what route was tested;
- what path health changed;
- what key/ledger/map join is needed;
- what proof/source pointer supports it;
- what boundary held;
- where the result returns.
