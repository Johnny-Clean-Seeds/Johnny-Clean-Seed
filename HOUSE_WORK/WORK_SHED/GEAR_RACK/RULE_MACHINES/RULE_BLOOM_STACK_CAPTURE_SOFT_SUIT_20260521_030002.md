# Rule Bloom and Stack Capture Soft Suit

## Status

Soft Suit / Provisional Active Growth Rule

## Worn Behavior

When an issue is addressed, capture it as active provisional rule/idea unless deliberately rejected.

When a stack appears, collect the whole stack before judging, compressing, or collapsing.

## Why This Exists

Some bloat is discovery pressure.

Small odd rules can expose the hidden family that later becomes a dense Broad-Bite rule.

## Immediate Use

Before responding after a correction, ask:

1. What issue/fix was addressed?
2. Is this an active provisional rule, held idea, or rejection?
3. Is there a stack behind it?
4. What should be collected before collapse?
5. What is the next live firing test?

## Boundary

Soft Suit only until proof supports promotion or collapse.

Do not treat this as doctrine.
Do not treat the whole stack as final truth.
Do not collapse early just to make it look clean.

## Proof

Proof is behavior change plus stack accounting.

A pass means the addressed issue does not stay as loose talk, and related stack items are not lost.
