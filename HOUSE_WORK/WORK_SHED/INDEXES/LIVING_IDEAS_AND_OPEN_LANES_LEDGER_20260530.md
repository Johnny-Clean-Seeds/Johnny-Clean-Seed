## Ledger rules

Living idea:
an idea with enough shape to keep alive, but not enough proof to promote.

Open lane:
a bounded next-work route with owner, proof need, stop condition, and return trigger.

Merge candidate:
an idea that should be connected to existing saved concepts instead of becoming a separate doctrine.

Parked:
not active, but intentionally retained with a return trigger.

Blocked:
not allowed to proceed until a missing proof/gate is supplied.
