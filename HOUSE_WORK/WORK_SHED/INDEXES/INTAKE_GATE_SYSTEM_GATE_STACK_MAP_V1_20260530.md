## Helper-readiness family

Context Carry:
keeps notes on a rope.

Helper Capability:
checks what the next helper can and cannot do.

Orientation Surface:
places small support orientation in the right files.

Handoff:
makes the next step readable.

Readiness Review:
decides whether the next actor can proceed.

Operator Approval:
blocks boundary-crossing until explicit approval.

Feedback Click:
captures working behavior without promotion.

Balance / Wobble:
slows or stops when the system leans wrong.

Escalation:
routes to operator, Code Gate, Word Gate, Proof Gate, Final Judge, parking, or blocked lane.

Runbook / Playbook:
captures minimum repeatable route after proof.

## Gate role warning

A gate map is not automation.

Use the map to orient the helper. Do not fire all gates for tiny tasks.
