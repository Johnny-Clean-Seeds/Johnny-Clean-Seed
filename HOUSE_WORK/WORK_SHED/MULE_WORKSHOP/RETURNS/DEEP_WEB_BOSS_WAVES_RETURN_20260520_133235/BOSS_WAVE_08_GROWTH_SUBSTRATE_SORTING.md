## Best Fix

Winner:
A3 Source-Ore Sorting Sheet.

Working shape:
For a pile of external ideas:
- Background: why the ideas were pulled.
- Current condition: what boss they touch.
- Gap: what local evidence still lacks.
- Candidate countermeasures: up to 10.
- Chosen countermeasure and why.
- Follow-up: apply/adapt/park/reject/needs proof.

## Proof To Myself

Why this wins:
A3 beats pure risk frameworks here because the job is not to govern AI risk or inspect every hazard. The job is to turn a messy idea field into one clear problem-solving story without prematurely promoting anything.

Why not NIST RMF:
NIST RMF is stronger for authority/risk control, but A3 is better for sorting and communicating a problem-solving path.

Disproof:
If the idea field contains safety-critical or high-risk items, FMEA/NIST risk screen should take over.

Verdict:
BEST CANDIDATE FOR WAVE 08 / USE FOR FUTURE IDEA PILES, NOT AS DOCTRINE.
