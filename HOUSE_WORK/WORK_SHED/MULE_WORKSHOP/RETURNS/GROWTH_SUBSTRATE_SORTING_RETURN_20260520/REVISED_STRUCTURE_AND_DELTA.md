## Revised structure

### Layer 1: Intake mount

Purpose:
- Stop source material from being interpreted before its lane, source, and authority state are known.

Required fields:
- source path;
- source hash when practical;
- source type;
- date/event;
- initial authority state;
- parent boss if present;
- old current-looking language flag.

### Layer 2: Trace split

Purpose:
- Prevent mixed material from becoming one blob.

Layers:
- content;
- process;
- proof;
- business;
- command;
- boundary.

### Layer 3: Boss/Ghost grouping

Purpose:
- Prevent symptom piles from driving route selection.

Fields:
- parent boss;
- ghost;
- child task;
- proof need;
- blocked moves.

### Layer 4: Proof scope

Purpose:
- Stop proof from overreaching.

Rule:
- Proof decides only the claim it proves.

Examples:
- A receipt proves the named action occurred.
- A mule return proves a worker output exists.
- A TODO proves a support surface exists.
- A method note proves a method was named, not fired.

### Layer 5: Placement view

Purpose:
- Make retrieval possible without creating a dashboard.

Views:
- Event Ledger for happened-and-proved records.
- Seed Bank for reusable but inactive ideas.
- Natural Trigger Index for event-based method firing.
- Business Risk View for trust/entry/handoff/product-story risks.
- Rejection Ledger for refused moves and why.
- Method Harness Index for methods, triggers, pass conditions, and No Rule King guards.
- Authority Boundary Map for command/support/source/blocked separation.

### Layer 6: Adoption state

Purpose:
- Preserve entry dignity and avoid premature promotion.

States:
- Active Authority.
- Support Evidence.
- Source Ore.
- Seed.
- Natural Trigger.
- Candidate After Proof.
- Rejected For This Scope.
- Blocked.
- Unknown.

## Delta

What changed from raw pile to revised structure:

- Root changed from "status/history is messy" to "mixed substrate lacks a consistent execution harness for authority, proof, trigger, and placement."
- Method changed from "make a clean list" to "mount, trace, expose, revise, and then place each item."
- Proof changed from "supporting the final answer" to "shaping the classification before conclusion."
- Business risk changed from a concern to a first-class sorting facet.
- Rejection changed from implicit avoidance to an explicit ledger.
- Status/history changed from possible steering surface to growth substrate.
- Mule output changed from possible recommendation pressure to support evidence.
- Good ideas changed from active pressure into Seed Bank candidates.
- Natural triggers replaced forced tests.
- Inspectable construction replaced polished synthesis.

## Revised final proposal in one sentence

Create no new crown: use a compact intake record and seven support views to classify growth substrate by source, proof, authority, boss, trigger, risk, rejection, and adoption state before any item can influence active work.
