## EXPOSE

Root pattern:
"Common sense" is not a single thing. It is a family name for several layers that cooperate in ordinary action and judgment.

The main false move is to ask for the one essence of common sense and then pick only one layer:

- philosophy says first principles;
- psychology says core/folk models;
- sociology says shared everyday methods;
- AI says everyday world knowledge and defeasible reasoning;
- pragmatics says common ground;
- decision science says bounded heuristics.

Each is real, but none is the entire thing alone.

Upgrade:

The strongest revised definition:

Common sense is a layered, situated, defeasible fit system: the capacity and background inventory by which a human quickly forms workable judgments about ordinary reality, other agents, language, norms, risk, and action under limited time, limited attention, and incomplete proof.
