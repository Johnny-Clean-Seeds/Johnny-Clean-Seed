# Hard Suit

## Purpose

Hard Suit is the proven operating pack.

It holds active pack items promoted from Soft Suit after they pass fit, neighbor, proof, and combined-effect checks.

Hard Suit is the only suit that may approve controlled dumps into active files.

## Hard pack size

Hard Suit may hold up to 10 active pack items.

Hard Suit does not need to fill 10.

The pack should stay only as large as clean work requires.

Support links are reference fuel, not worn pack items.

They help the suit think, but they do not count as active Hard Suit pack slots unless promoted into the active pack through proof.

Learning rules, proof receipts, maps, templates, indexes, and source notes may be linked as support references, but they do not count as active Hard Suit pack slots unless promoted into the active pack.

If an active pack item no longer helps, conflicts with the pack, duplicates another item, or becomes stale, it may be moved out through proof and Respect Thy Neighbor checks.

## Linked Soft Suit testing

Hard Suit links to Soft Suit during candidate testing.

Soft Suit candidates are judged against the current Hard Suit pack before promotion.

A candidate should not enter Hard Suit merely because it works alone. It must work cleanly with the proven pack.

## What can enter Hard Suit

A Soft Suit item can enter Hard Suit only when:

- it fits the core
- it blesses the model without drift
- Respect Thy Neighbor passes
- proof shows the item works
- combined-effect testing passes
- the item has a clear job and lane
- the item is not duplicating or overloading another helper

## Controlled dump rule

Only Hard Suit active pack items may approve a controlled dump into active files.

A controlled dump means moving a proven suited item into an active guide, rule, workflow, or operating file.

Before a controlled dump into active files, check:

- target lane
- neighbor rules
- proof
- risk of bloat
- whether a pointer is enough
- whether the item should stay in Hard Suit only

## Close condition

A Hard Suit item remains active only while it continues to fit the core and help the model.
