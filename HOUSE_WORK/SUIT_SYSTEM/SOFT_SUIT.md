# Soft Suit

## Purpose

Soft Suit is the trial-fit suit.

It holds candidate helpers, rules, patterns, fixes, bosses, and ideas while they are being tested for fit.

Soft Suit is not final authority.

## Hard limit

Soft Suit may hold no more than 3 active bosses at one time.

Soft Suit may test 1, 2, or 3 active bosses.

Soft Suit does not need to fill all 3 slots.

If more than 3 boss candidates appear, park the rest as next-cycle candidates with status and priority.

## Linked Hard Suit testing

Soft Suit does not test candidates in isolation.

Soft Suit tests new candidate bosses, helpers, or fixes while linked to the current Hard Suit.

This lets the assistant see whether a candidate:

- strengthens the proven pack
- conflicts with the proven pack
- duplicates an existing Hard Suit item
- overloads the active loadout
- needs a companion
- should be parked
- should be promoted

## What can enter Soft Suit

Soft Suit may hold:

- candidate fixes
- candidate rules
- candidate word keys
- candidate helper/workers
- candidate maps
- candidate patterns
- candidate adoption ideas
- current boss candidates
- test loadout items

## Entry gate

Before entering Soft Suit, an item needs:

- name
- lane
- job
- reason it may help
- neighbor lanes touched
- proof needed
- status

## Soft Suit status labels

Use:

- candidate
- testing
- active boss
- companion-needed
- proof-needed
- parking-needed
- failed-fit
- ready-for-hard-suit

## Exit paths

A candidate may leave Soft Suit as:

- promoted to Hard Suit
- parked
- failed-fit
- companion-needed
- proof-needed
- moved out as not needed

## Soft Suit rule

Soft Suit tests fit against the attached Hard Suit pack.

It does not dump directly into active files.

Only Hard Suit may approve a controlled dump into active files.

## Close condition

A Soft Suit item may move to Hard Suit only after fit, neighbor, proof, and combined-effect checks.
