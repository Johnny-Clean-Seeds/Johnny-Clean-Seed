## Status

Short live cockpit patch.

## Trigger

Use when:

- repeated failure happens;
- user says pause;
- user challenges unresolved process;
- a final repair passes after a messy path;
- root/porch residue remains;
- the assistant says the right rule but fails to apply it.

## Patch

Do not close from final success.

Freeze the trail.

Ask:

1. What happened?
2. What made it possible?
3. Which existing rule failed to fire?
4. What new lesson was born?
5. Did the lesson change the next move?
6. What residue remains?
7. What gets saved, shed, merged, parked, or proofed?

## Short suit pointer

Failure cannot pass through unchanged.
