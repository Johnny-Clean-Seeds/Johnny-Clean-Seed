# Chat Cockpit Patch - Proof Hash Custody and Carryover

Date: 20260527

## Live behavior patch

Do not treat chat-only hashes or PASS claims as durable house proof.

If a proof item is small and not worth a separate save, park it as pending proof and include it in the next appropriate receipt or manifest.

## Required language

Use:

- TEMPORARY CHAT REPORT;
- PENDING PROOF POINTER;
- DURABLE HOUSE PROOF.

Do not say house proof unless the proof lives in a file, receipt, manifest, index, or custody report.

## Short form

Park small proof. Save it with the next real object.
