## Sequence Read

1. LocalBot begins as a small controlled local runner. It proves the tiny loop before expanding.
2. LocalBot hardens by separating completion from correctness and preserving evidence before repair.
3. LocalBot closes practical use and parks weak lanes instead of dragging unfinished ideas through the active core.
4. CleanMilkChecker repeats the same shape in seed form: one input, one verdict, invalid/yield paths, recovery, closeout.
5. The house later grows rope, washer, gate, note-weight, tug, runner, burn, and frame/freedom rules around the same skeleton.
6. The GPT_RESULTS sequence shows that tightening the agent improves output until the model begins to become rigid. The clean answer is stage-specific frame, not maximum frame everywhere.
