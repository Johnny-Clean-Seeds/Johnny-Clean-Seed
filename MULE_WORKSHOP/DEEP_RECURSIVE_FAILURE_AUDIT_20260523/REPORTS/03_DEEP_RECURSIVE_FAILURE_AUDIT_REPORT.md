## 9. Root Cause Judgment

Root cause:

The system has enough rules. It lacks a consistently enforced recursive mirror latch at the point where work starts, changes state, receives a new object, or claims completion.

The project knows the right thing, but the knowledge often lands as saved material, receipt, support rule, or parking note. It does not always reflect into:

- the active boss,
- the entry surface,
- neighboring rules,
- child behaviors,
- TODO/queue,
- parking return,
- delivery surface,
- home/path,
- source custody,
- mule boundary,
- proof receipt,
- Final Judge,
- Git/local split.

In the user's words: the mirrors exist, but they are not all facing each other.
